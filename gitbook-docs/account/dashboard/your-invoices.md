# Your Invoices

After you purchase a license for Super Forms, you will receive your invoice per E-mail. It will be attached as a PDF file.

All invoices can be found via **Super Forms > Licenses > \[Billing]** via your WordPress Dashboard.

{% hint style="warning" %}
In order to view your Invoices you will have to login with your Super Forms account first!
{% endhint %}
